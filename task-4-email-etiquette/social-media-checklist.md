# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ 5 Do's - Responsible Usage
- **Post academic achievements** - Share projects, certifications, hackathon wins
- **Use professional handles** - for example use @AdityaNegiVIT instead of @adityacool123
- **Engage positively** - Comment thoughtfully, cite sources when sharing
- **Set privacy settings** - Limit personal posts related to friends/close connections
- **Network professionally** - Connect with alumni, professors, recruiters

## ❌ 5 Don'ts - Avoid These Mistakes
- **Never share sensitive info** - ID proofs, bank details, address, phone
- **No posting during class** - Respect academic environment and focus
- **Avoid public arguments** - Use DMs for disagreements, don't engage trolls
- **Skip slang/abuse** - Professional tone builds credibility with recruiters
- **Don't overshare personal life** - Party photos with location tags attract wrong attention
