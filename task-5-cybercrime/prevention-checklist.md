# Stay Safe Online Checklist
**For College Students in India**  
*Task 5: Cybercrime Prevention Guide (CSE0001 Digital Literacy)*

## ✅ 10 Actionable Prevention Tips

1. **Verify before you click** - Hover over email links to check real URLs before clicking
2. **Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on email, college portal, and social accounts
3. **Use strong, unique passwords** (12+ characters, mix of letters/numbers/symbols)
4. **Keep phone/software updated** - Enable auto-updates for security patches
5. **Avoid public WiFi for banking** - Use mobile data for UPI transactions

### 💰 UPI/Financial Safety (Mandatory)
6. **Never share UPI PIN** - Not even with family/friends; banks never ask for it
7. **Use Virtual Payment Address (VPA)** instead of phone number for UPI payments

8. **Download apps only from official stores** - Google Play/App Store, avoid WhatsApp forwards

## 🚨 If You Face Cybercrime
- **Report immediately:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) 
- **Call helpline:** **1930** (24x7 National Cyber Crime Helpline)

## 📱 Quick Student Checklist

- 2 Factor Authentication must be enabled on all accounts
- Software auto-updates ON
- Strong unique passwords
- Verify links before clicking
- UPI PIN never shared
- Only official app stores
- Public WiFi = No banking
